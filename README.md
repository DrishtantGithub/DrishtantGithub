I am an Electrical Engineer and Machine Learning researcher currently pursuing graduate studies in Electrical Engineering (Control and Computing) at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay.

My work lies at the intersection of deep learning, signal processing, and optimization, with a particular focus on renewable energy forecasting and intelligent control systems. I am interested in developing technically rigorous models that combine modern machine learning with classical engineering principles.

My research experience includes the design of novel deep learning architectures, integration of explainable AI techniques, and application of machine learning to real-world energy forecasting problems. I am committed to advancing impactful technological solutions through research and open-source collaboration.

## Research and Publications

### Enhancing Solar Energy Forecasting: 1D CNN With Explainable AI Techniques  
Accepted at INGARSS 2024

Key contributions include:

- Development of a novel 1D Convolutional Neural Network architecture incorporating Residual Inception and Squeeze-and-Excitation blocks.
- Achieved a 6.56% reduction in Mean Absolute Error compared to the top model in the AMS Solar Energy Prediction Contest.

- Research aligned with Sustainable Development Goals related to clean energy and smart grid forecasting.
